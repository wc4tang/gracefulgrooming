- Our Story

Welcome to Graceful Grooming—Kingston East. With over 25 years of experience we can service all your pets' grooming needs. Here your pet is truly #1. We are a family run business located in the East end of the beautiful city of Kingston Ontario.

Accepting all sizes of dog and cat breeds we are a one stop shop. Keeping our prices as reasonable as possible enables everyone the ability to keep their pet clean and happy. From nail trims to full service packages there is something for everyone. We look forward to serving you in the near future.
